Sonicwave Records

In an electrifying collaboration, rock band SonicWave has teamed up with the pulse-pounding rhythm game Geometry Dash to create an unforgettable experience. SonicWave's high-energy soundtracks will now be featured in Geometry Dash's challenging levels, perfectly synchronizing the band's infectious beats with the game's lightning-fast gameplay. As players navigate the game's demanding obstacles, SonicWave's music will fuel their focus and adrenaline, creating a truly immersive experience. This partnership promises to push the boundaries of music and gaming, delivering an exhilarating fusion of sound and speed that will leave fans of both SonicWave and Geometry Dash breathless.
