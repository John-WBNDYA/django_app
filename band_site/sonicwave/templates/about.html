About Sonicwave

Sonicwave is an electrifying rock band known for their dynamic performances and powerful sound. Formed in 2021, the band has captivated audiences around the world with their unique blend of classic and modern rock influences. The group consists of five artists, Jared Mcflinn, Mack Solomons, Eathan Murray, Ben Davids and Antony Sullivan ,each bringing their own flair and talent to create a truly unforgettable musical experience. Sonicwave has won a wide range of awards and nominations. In 2022, they won the best rock album of the year award for their album, Nexus Point. Later that year, they were nominated for the best rock band of 2022. They have also been named the best rock band of 2023.
